Up and running in three steps

No servers, no specialist installers — if you can mount a reader, you can run Sentritec.

  1. 01

    Mount the reader

    Fix any Sentritec reader to the door and power it on — it connects straight to the cloud.

  2. 02

    Add it in the dashboard

    The reader appears in Sentritec Cloud. Name it, assign it to a site, and you're set.

  3. 03

    Grant access

    Invite people, set schedules and permissions — doors open the moment you hit save.
