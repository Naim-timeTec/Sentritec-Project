The Evolution of Security Solutions

About SentriTec

SentriTec is a cloud-based access control platform that helps modern buildings manage doors, users, credentials, and access activity from one dashboard.

Our journey began with FingerTec in 2010, delivering biometric access control and time attendance through reliable local server-based systems. With TimeTec, that foundation expanded into cloud-based solutions.

Today, SentriTec brings access control into the cloud with smart readers, mobile access, centralized management, and scalable deployment for modern buildings.

Our Evolution

2010

FingerTec

Our foundation began with biometric access control and time attendance solutions, helping organizations manage identity, attendance, and physical access through reliable local server-based systems.

2015

TimeTec

Building on that experience, TimeTec introduced cloud-based solutions for human resources, property management, parking, security, and IoT.

2026

SentriTec

SentriTec brings this experience into a dedicated cloud access control platform, combining smart hardware, mobile access, flexible credentials, access schedules, device monitoring, and activity records in one system.

Our Mission

To make building access simpler, smarter, and more scalable through cloud-based access control technology.
