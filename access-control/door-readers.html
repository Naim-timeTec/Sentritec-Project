Choose your credentials.

Sentritec door readers work with low-frequency proximity cards, high-frequency NFC credentials like DESFire EV3, QR codes, PIN numbers, and encrypted mobile credentials.

Plug it in and you're online.

Setting up a secure door is now as simple as plugging in a single PoE cable. It carries both power and data, so the reader turns on and instantly connects to your software the moment it is plugged in.

No need for controllers.

Say goodbye to those metal boxes hiding in your utility closets and the messy jungle of wires that comes with them. We put the controller right inside the reader itself, saving you space, cutting down hardware costs, and keeping everything clean.

It works even without internet.

Your doors shouldn't lock you out just because your internet goes down. Because the reader holds all the access info locally, your team can still tap, wave, or scan their way into the building completely normally, even during a total internet outage.

Grow at your own pace.

Whether you are setting up one back door today or adding offices across the globe next year, expanding your system is effortless. You never have to calculate if you have enough room for more hardware. You just plug in another reader whenever you need it.

Issue temporary keys instantly.

Giving guests or contractors access is entirely hassle-free. You can quickly generate and send a temporary key as a time-bound QR code to anyone before they arrive. When they show up, they simply flash the code on their phone screen in front of the reader to get instant, authorized entry during their scheduled window.

Double up on protection for critical areas.

Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for your most sensitive assets. This requires users to present both their mobile app credential and type in a unique PIN code on the touch keypad before the door will unlock.
