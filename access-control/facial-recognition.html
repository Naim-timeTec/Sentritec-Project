Unlocks before you even reach the door

Face Core is built for high-traffic environments, recognizing authorized users in under a second. It detects you naturally as you walk up, keeping your team moving through busy entryways without bottlenecks.

Foolproof depth-sensing

Rest easy knowing your spaces are safe. Face Core pairs its lens with a 3D infrared time-of-flight depth sensor. By checking actual depth and physical contours, it easily differentiates between a real person and a spoof attempt.

Privacy first, by design.

Security shouldn't come at the cost of personal privacy. Face Core converts facial features into a secure numerical representation, and all credential matching and authentication are performed locally on the device, with data encryption safeguarding data both at rest and in transit.

No extra controllers. No internet worries.

Installation is as simple as plugging in a single PoE cable for both power and connectivity. Because the access controller is built directly inside the device and stores all database info locally, your doors remain fully operational and secure even during a total network or internet outage.
